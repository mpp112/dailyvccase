---
name: china-comparable
description: Find, score, and verify Chinese comparable companies for an overseas startup, including financing history, legal investment entities, investor brands or managers, and China-market applicability. Use when a case needs Chinese direct or adjacent comparables.
---

Follow `SOP.md` sections 6–9.

Process:

1. Build a problem profile from customer, payer, pain, product, technology, business model, go-to-market, deployment, and regulation.
2. Create at least five candidates.
3. Score customer 20, pain 25, product 20, business model 15, go-to-market 10, and stage 10.
4. Use `direct` only at 75+, `adjacent` at 55–74, otherwise `reference`.
5. State why comparable and why not comparable.
6. Verify financing rounds and no more than three core investors.
7. Distinguish legal investment entity, manager, and public brand.
8. Do not treat corporate registration changes as financing without additional evidence.

Return structured Comparable and FinancingEvent records plus unresolved gaps.
